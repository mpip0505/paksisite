About Us

Your trusted partner in defense and maritime trading

Strategic Defense Trading Partner

Established in 2000, Paksi Laksana Sdn Bhd (PLSB) is a premier trading and service company headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, with a branch office in Lumut, Perak, strategically located near the Royal Malaysian Navy base.

Our team is predominantly composed of retired naval officers with extensive expertise in logistics, engineering, and specialized equipment supply. This unique background gives us unparalleled operational knowledge of naval and marine requirements.

We are strategically positioned to support Malaysia's defense sector with a focus on delivering high-quality products and services that meet the requirements of military and maritime operations.
